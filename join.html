Prospective Student

We're actively looking for talented candidates who are excited about doing fun research to join us. Openning post-doctoral fellow, Ph.D., research assistant (RA), internship and visiting positions are available. If fitting to the following cases, you are welcomed to drop us an email introducing yourself.

Post-doctoral Fellow: We hope you have a solid understanding of experience in NLP/AI and publications in at least one journal and three top conferences.
Ph.D. Programme in CUHK SEEM: Applicants should make sure to apply for CUHK SEEM Ph.D. program. Please note the deadline for application. Outstanding candidates are encouraged to apply for Hong Kong Ph.D. Fellowship.
Research Assistant/Intern and Visitors: 1) If you are currently studying a postgraduate degree in a non-local university and expected to graduate on or after June 2024, you are welcome to apply for a 6-12 months internship; 2) Fresh graduates are encouraged to apply for research assistant positions. Salary will be commensurate with qualifications and experience; 3) Ph.D. students from other labs are welcome to engage in a visiting experience with our team.

Contact Information

In your email, please try to include the following information:

1. Title: "Prospective Student: Your Name - Your Affliation";
2. Briefly describe your (1) education background; (2) research publications and experiences in areas related to NLP, ML and data mining;
3. Include a PDF version of your CV.

Applicants can drop the email to kf_prj [at] se [dot] cuhk [dot] edu [dot] hk. Welcome to join us!
